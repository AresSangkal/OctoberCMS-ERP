GCC Milestone

GCC was launched in 2006 as the merger of the four constituent companies which included the construction arms of Al-Tamimi FAFCO-PETCON (Abdullah Fouad), Abdullah H. Al-Shuwayer, Al-Tamimi and Al-Jabr Trading.
